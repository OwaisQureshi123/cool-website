Vision & Mission

Empowering Personal Growth Through Compassionate and Holistic Counseling

At Magnolia Conselling PLLC, we aim to empower individuals to reach their full potential through compassionate, evidence-based counseling. We envision a world where everyone feels valued and supported on their journey to self-discovery and well-being. By prioritizing cultural competence, collaborative goal-setting, and holistic care, we strive to create transformative experiences that inspire meaningful change and improve our clients' quality of life.

  1. Empowering Your Unique Strengths: I am committed to helping individuals unlock their inherent strengths and unique qualities, empowering them to realize their full potential and achieve personal growth..
  2. Person-Centered and Evidence-Based Approaches: I believe that you are the primary architect of your life and goals. My counseling approach is rooted in Person-Centered Therapy, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR). Additionally, I may incorporate other therapeutic methods as needed, such as trauma-informed therapy, Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Narrative Therapy, and alternative approaches including mindfulness interventions and Expressive Art Therapy.
  3. Commitment to Cultural Competence: My therapeutic approach is guided by Section A.4.b of the American Counseling Association's Code of Ethics. I am committed to respecting the diversity, values, beliefs, and cultures of my clients, ensuring that I do not impose my own values or beliefs. I actively seek to enhance my understanding through ongoing learning and training about my clients' cultural backgrounds in order to provide culturally competent services.
  4. Experience with a Range of Issues: I have extensive experience assisting clients in overcoming a range of challenges, including abuse by narcissistic parents, domestic violence (both current and historical), post-divorce issues, anxiety disorders, depression, gender identity concerns, religious trauma (both current and historical), adjustment difficulties, self-esteem issues, communication problems, relationship difficulties, and life transitions. Additionally, I offer specialized support for women’s issues, life transitions, and highly functional individuals.
  5. Creating a Safe and Supportive Space: I am dedicated to leveraging my knowledge and expertise to establish a comfortable, open, respectful, and non-judgmental environment for my clients.
